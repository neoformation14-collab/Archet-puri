About Me

Who I Am

Archet Puri is an accomplished professional in the camera and electrical department of the Indian film industry, with extensive experience working on major Bollywood productions and independent projects.

With a career spanning various roles from Assistant Camera to Cinematographer, I bring a comprehensive understanding of the entire camera department pipeline and a dedication to visual storytelling.

My nickname in the industry is "RDX" - a testament to the energy and impact I bring to every project.
